Configuring the environment

Here we will initialize a firebase app to serve as your backend storage for the application data.

Follow the steps below to configure the application environment.

  • You can use your current Google account or open a new Google account for this purpose. Either way, once authenticated go to firebase.com in the same browser you are authenticated with your Google account
  • On the right top corner click on GO TO CONSOLE
  • If you havent yet, add a project and once into the project click the link on the top left corner Project Overview then select Project settings
  • From the 3 buttons at the lowest portion of the page select the one reading 'Add Firebase to your web app'
  • On the window that pops up Click on the 'Copy' buttton on the right on it
